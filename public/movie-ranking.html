Top 3 movies of all time

According to Hassan Abbud

Number 1: A silent voice

Really good anime movie, talks about a Middle School bully who later learns about the wrong in his actions and tries to mend it. It touches on topics such as depression friendship and forgivness. Persoally I think is beautiful

Number 2: Your name

From Makoto Shinkai, his most famous work Your Name is a story about a high school boy and girl that exchange bodies mysteriously and they have to figure out how to live each others lifes while keeping their own as it should be. Romantic, dramatic and funny. Really good movie.

Number 3: Adventures of TinTin

Great movie all around, especially the comic parts. It has a good premise and gives thrills and funny moments to the viewer throughout the movie.
